-KisanBazar is a digital B2B market solution that brings together Farmers and Industrial Buyers.

-We drive agricultural transactions through our digital platform in combination with our service partnership network.KisanBazar accommodates online payments between buyer and seller,

-KisanBazar does not buy or sell crops and is not a broker. Instead, we offer you the ability to effortlessly market your crop via our platform.

HOW IT WORKS

  • 1. Sign-up to the platform
  • 2. Create your offer or bid
  • 3. Provide us with crop characteristics & details
  • 4. Post your seller offer or buyer bid
